#  Discussion

## Interpretation

The RNA-Seq analysis revealed significant transcriptional changes between treated and control samples.

The observed separation in PCA indicates strong biological differences between conditions.

---

## Biological Insights

- Several genes were significantly upregulated or downregulated
- Enrichment analysis suggests involvement of key biological pathways

---

## Limitations

- Small sample size may limit statistical power
- Potential technical variation between samples

---

## Future Work

- Validate key genes using qPCR
- Extend analysis to other conditions or datasets

## Conclusion

This study demonstrates the effectiveness of RNA-Seq analysis using Galaxy for identifying and interpreting gene expression changes.
