#  Results

## Overview

The RNA-Seq analysis identified genes that are significantly differentially expressed between treated and untreated samples.

---

## Quality Control

- Most samples showed high-quality reads (Phred score ≥ 30)
- Minimal adapter contamination observed

---

## Alignment

- High alignment rates achieved using HISAT2
- Majority of reads mapped uniquely to the reference genome

---

## Differential Expression

- Significant DEGs identified using DESeq2
- Both upregulated and downregulated genes observed

---

## Visualization

### PCA Plot
- Clear separation between treated and untreated samples

### Volcano Plot
- Highlights statistically significant genes

### Heatmap
- Shows clustering of top differentially expressed genes

---

## Functional Enrichment

- Enriched GO terms identified for biological processes
- KEGG pathways reveal affected molecular pathways

---

## Summary

The results demonstrate significant transcriptional differences between conditions, indicating strong biological effects of treatment.
